Note Heads

Drumline notation uses specific note heads to show different sounds you can produce on a snare drum.

Snare Note Heads

Hit

Double Stop

Rim Shot

Ping

Stick Shot

Stick hits the other stick on the head.

Rim Knock

left stick lays across drum. lift butt-end while keeping the tip of the stick on the drum.

Rim

Rim-only sound.

Backstick / Visual
